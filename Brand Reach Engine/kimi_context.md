## 5. Signals to Engineer

### E-E-A-T Signals (YMYL-Critical)
- [ ] Medical/expert review of all health-related content (orthopedic doctors, sleep therapists)
- [ ] Real author bylines with credentials (not "admin" or "team")
- [ ] Clear return policy, warranty, trial period prominently displayed
- [ ] Genuine customer reviews with schema markup
- [ ] HTTPS, clear pricing, transparent shipping timelines

### User Behavior Signals
- [ ] Minimize pogo-sticking (match search intent precisely)
- [ ] Increase dwell time (embedded video, interactive tools, long-form depth)
- [ ] Optimize CTR with benefit-driven titles ("Doctor Recommended," "100-Night Trial")

### Technical Signals
- [ ] LCP < 2.5s (image optimization critical for mattress sites)
- [ ] Mobile-first flawless experience
- [ ] Complete schema stack: Product, Review, AggregateRating, FAQPage, Organization, LocalBusiness, VideoObject, BreadcrumbList

### Brand Signals
- [ ] Track brand search volume monthly
- [ ] Offline campaigns (TV, Metro, Instagram) to spike brand search
- [ ] Entity building in Google's Knowledge Graph

## 8. The Competitive Moat

Most mattress brands compete on discounting and thin content. To dominate:

| Dimension | Typical Rank 6–7 Brand | Rank 1 Strategy |
|-----------|------------------------|-----------------|
| **Content** | Thin descriptions, generic blogs | Doctor-reviewed, medically accurate, localized, multilingual |
| **Technical** | Slow, image-heavy, missing schema | Sub-2.5s LCP, full schema, flawless mobile UX |
| **Authority** | Few backlinks, no brand search | Publisher partnerships + original data PR + rising brand search |
| **Trust** | Hidden policies, fake-looking reviews | Transparent pricing, 100-night trial, verified reviews, expert endorsements |

**The Flywheel:**
Better Technical → Higher Rankings → More Traffic → More Reviews → Stronger E-E-A-T → Even Higher Rankings → More Brand Search → Google Entity Recognition → Market Leadership
