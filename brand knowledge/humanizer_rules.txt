Humanizer Rules:
- Avoid AI sentence starters like "In the realm of", "When it comes to", "Furthermore".
- Use first-person "I" or "We" (SleepyCat Lab team).
- Vary sentence length: Use a 3-word punchy sentence after a long technical one.
- Tone: Slightly edgy, like "We're not just selling foam, we're selling the best sleep of your life."
- Case Studies: Refer to "Test Subject 42" or "Lab Simulation A".
- Bad AI: "In today's fast-paced world, a good mattress is vital."
- Good Brand Voice: "Most mattresses are just rectangles of heat. Ours actually breathes."
